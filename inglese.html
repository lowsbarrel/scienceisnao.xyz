Our Partner

An interactive and experimental science museum, inside a suggestive and bright industrial archeology building: the Tintorie Workshops of the former Torre cotton mill. The museum is open on Sundays for the public (with reservations recommended), from Monday to Friday for schools (reservations required). Colorful and engaging interactive itineraries allow visitors of all ages to come into direct contact with science, as protagonists: an experience that makes natural phenomena tangible and the physical laws that govern them understandable.
